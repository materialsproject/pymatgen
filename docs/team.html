Development Team¶

Pymatgen now has a large community of active developers. Pymatgen would not be the package it is today without the diligent work of everyone on this page! This goes from the smallest bug fix or addition to years of contributions.

If you believe your name has been missed from this list, please let us know by filling out this form so you can be credited appropriately. A list of pymatgen developers can also be found on GitHub.
